COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much do lessons cost?

Due to the flexibility of the courses, lessons are charged individually, so that they can continue until the student fully understands all material. Each 40 minute sessions costs $20 US dollars, but we are open to negotiating the price and the length of the lessons.

What is the format of the lessons?

The 1-to-1 lessons are conducted online, at a time that works for both the students and teachers. While the content is giving in the form of a slideshow presentation, each lesson has one or more hands-on activities where the students will complete small projects by writing code.

Is this only for children and teens?

No, our courses are for anyone who wants to learn the basics of these topics. They are designed to be simple and taught by high schoolers and collage students, so if you want a comprehensive or advanced course on a subject it may be better to look elsewhere.

How do I enroll?

If you want to get started with our lessons, please email us at hnasheralneam@gmail.com with the course you want to take, and what times you would be available for lessons.
